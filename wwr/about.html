White Water Rafting Company

Our team of certified guides is passionate about river stewardship and guest safety. We offer guided rafting trips for all experience levels, from gentle family floats to challenging white-water runs. Every trip includes professional instruction, modern safety equipment, and a focus on making your adventure fun, memorable, and safe.

History

Founded in 2005 by a group of local river guides, White Water Rafting Company began as a small weekend operation. Over the years we've grown into a full-service outfitter while keeping a strong commitment to conservation, community partnerships, and outdoor education. Our mission is to share the river while protecting it for future generations.
